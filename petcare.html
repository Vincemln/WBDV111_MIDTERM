Pet Care Tips

🥗 Balanced Diet

Feed your pet age-appropriate food and avoid table scraps. Fresh water should always be available.

🏃 Daily Exercise

Dogs need at least 30 min of activity daily. Cats benefit from interactive toys and play sessions.

🩺 Regular Check-ups

Schedule vet visits every 6–12 months to catch issues early and keep vaccinations up to date.

🛁 Grooming Routine

Brush your pet regularly, trim nails monthly, and clean ears to prevent infections.

❤️ Mental Stimulation

Puzzles, training games, and social interaction keep your pet mentally sharp and happy.

🏠 Safe Environment

Keep toxic plants, foods, and small objects out of reach. Secure electrical cords and open windows.
